Pixel Protection
Pixel Noise (LSB)Medium
Flips the least-significant bits of pixels across the image. Invisible to the eye but defeats pixel-level forensic analysis and watermarking.
Sensor Fingerprint Defeat (PRNU)Medium
Every camera has a unique sensor noise pattern. This layer overwrites it with a structured noise field that mimics a generic device, making it impossible to identify your phone or camera.
Color ShiftMedium
Applies a micro gamma adjustment to alter the tonal fingerprint. Sites use color histograms to cluster images from the same source. Hardlocked so the shift is imperceptible.
AI / Recognition ConfusionMedium
Injects adversarial gradient patterns that confuse AI recognition and reverse image search engines without being visible to humans.
Encoder & Frequency
JPEG Encoder Fingerprint SpoofMedium
Every camera app and editor has a unique JPEG encoding signature. This layer replaces it at the DCT block level, making the image appear to come from a different, generic encoder.
Frequency Domain NoiseMedium
Adds three superimposed sinusoidal noise carriers at different frequencies and phases. Destroys forensic frequency histogram analysis that platforms use to identify images from the same source. Multiple carriers make it far harder to reverse than a single pattern.
Geometric & Identity
Facial Geometry WarpMedium
Applies a sub-pixel sinusoidal displacement field across the image. Shifts pixel positions by less than 1px — completely invisible — but defeats facial geometry matching that measures exact distances between facial landmarks.
Batch Pattern BreakMedium
Adds unique per-pixel Gaussian noise to each channel so photos from the same shoot cannot be forensically clustered together. Essential when posting multiple images from the same session.
Chroma Fingerprint SpoofMedium
Subtly shifts the Cb and Cr colour channels independently, mimicking the chroma rendering pipeline of a different camera. Luma (brightness) is unchanged so the image looks identical. Breaks colour-based device fingerprinting.
